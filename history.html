Brief History

GHIMA was formed on 19th October, 2012 in Accra by pioneers led by:

  • Mr. James Addo
  • Mr. Asem Livingstone
  • Mr. Alandu Augustine
  • Mr. Ken Gbeve
  • Mr. James Makpa
  • Mr. Atsu Ayi
  • Mr. Garnett Brown
  • ... and other key members.

The association is a member of the International Federation of Health Information Management Association (IFHIMA) and the Ghana Federation of Allied Health Professions (GFAHP).

Association Structures

GHIMA is made up of several decision-making structures:

  • AGM (Annual General Meeting) as the highest decision-making platform.
  • NEC (National Executive Committee) as the second highest decision-making platform. It is comprised of constitutionally selected stakeholders for major decisions in the absence of AGM.
  • A seven-member active national officers team provides leadership.
  • A five-member regional officers team is present in all the 16 regions of Ghana.

Membership

GHIMA boasts a membership strength of 5,000 professionals and practitioners spread across the length and breadth of Ghana.
